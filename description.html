Decription of the Game

This game is a board game.It can be played in Single-Player.The player takes guesses and fire shots to hit the battleships on the grid when all the battleships are been shot the game will announce the player wins the game and counts the amount of shots taken by the player.

Instruction of the game

The game requires the player to guess the position of each battleships on a 7x7 squares grid. To place a hit on one of the grids squares, click on one of the squares. The player's guesses would either be hit or miss, both of which would have outputs to tell the player if they guessed right or not.If the player click on the square that have no battleship the square will become grey color and will show a list of words "Doh! You missed. Shoot again!" .When the player click on the square that have a battleship the square will become red and will show "Woohoo! It's a hit!". When the player successfully destroys a ship, the caption box would output a caption saying what type of ship did the player destroyed. When the game is finished, the web page would output an alert box that congratulates the player and letting the player know the number of shots the player used. The caption box would also output a caption that lets the player know if they wish to restart the game they must refresh the page.
